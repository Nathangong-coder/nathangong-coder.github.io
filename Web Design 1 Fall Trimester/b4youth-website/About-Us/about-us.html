What is B4Youth?

B4Youth is a non-profit charity dedicated to teaching kids about bridge. Bridge is an interesting and useful game, and we want to share this knowledge and our passion with the game with a next generation. The average bridge player's age is 71, and hopefully, with a next generation of bridge players, we can lower that average. We also hope that by teaching more people bridge, bridge will also not be stereotyped as an "old person's game".

This is from the TV show Cobra Kai, where Johnny Lawrence tells John Kreese to leave the Miyagi dojo with his partner, Terry Silver. Johnny Lawrence calls Terry Silver old, by referencing him as his "bridge partner", a game stereotyped for old people.
