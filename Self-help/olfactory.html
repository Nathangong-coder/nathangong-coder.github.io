This is my olfactory self-help page, with all the smells that keep me at rest

Some smells that keep me at rest:

  • 粉蒸肉, otherwise known as steamed pork and potatoes
  • Cut grass
  • Trees, grass, or anything fresh
  • Any freshly baked desserts (eg: brownies, cookies, cakes, pies, etc)
  • Fruity scents (not air freshener, that makes me dizzy and the opposite of helping)
  • Any good flower scent
  • New tennis balls
