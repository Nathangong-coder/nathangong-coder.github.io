This is my auditory self-help page

What kinds of sounds help me:

  • Music that includes Sunflower, Happier, Lose (By KSI), Don't Play, Classical (bach, mozart, beethoven, chopin, etc), etc.
  • Nature sounds that includes birds chirping, ocean waves, calm breezes, woodpecker pecking, rainstorm sounds, etc.
  • Talking and just self-help podcasts and other self-help things that you can listen to can also help me and motivate me.
