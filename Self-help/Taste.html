This is my taste self-help page

Some tastes that comfort me:

  • The taste of 粉蒸肉, or almost anything else that my grandpa makes for dinner
  • Desserts (croquemboche, brownies, cakes, ice cream, chocolate mousse, donuts, etc)
  • Anything brunchy or savory that's good (Tacos, Burritoes, Bacon & Eggs, Eggs Benedict, Hash Browns, etc
  • Most types of fruit that hasn't gone bad (apples, mangoes, pineapples, oranges, strawberries, etc)
  • Anything else that tastes good (savory and sweet foods, some bitter foods, some sour foods, etc)
