What is B4Youth?

B4Youth is a non-profit charity dedicated to teaching kids about bridge. Bridge is an interesting and useful game, and we want to share this knowledge and our passion with the game with a next generation. The average bridge player's age is 71, and hopefully, with a next generation of bridge players, we can lower that age.
