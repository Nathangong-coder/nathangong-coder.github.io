But what is suspicious and suspiciousness?

Suspicious is an adjective that describes the state of being suspicious. It means having doubts or mistrust toward someone or something. For example, if someone is acting nervously or evasively, they may be viewed as suspicious. Suspicious behavior can also include things like avoiding eye contact, refusing to answer questions, or trying to cover up or hide something.
